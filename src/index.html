Research & Presentations

Agentic AI and everything you need to know about them

Pranali Gole, Gargi Sathe | University of Illinois at Chicago, Spring 2025

This technical overview defines agentic AI systems via six core components—perception, planning, memory, reasoning, communication, and action—providing a unified framework for both one‑step and sequential inference models . It surveys agent‑to‑human, agent‑to‑agent, and agent‑to‑tool communication modalities and introduces the CAMEL role‑playing framework for multi‑agent collaboration . Finally, it demonstrates the ReAct function‑calling paradigm and presents the AI Agent Index’s analysis of 67 real‑world systems—covering developer types, safety disclosures, and governance challenges—before compiling key external resources.

Gargi Sathe, Aneesh Shamraj, Aditya Surve, Nahush Patil, Kumkum Saxena. | International Conference on Intelligent Systems Design and Applications, pp. 468–479, 2023.
